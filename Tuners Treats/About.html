About Me

Hi! I'm Josephine Turner, a professional baker with a love for creating unique, artisanal baked goods. After years of working in a bakery, I've decided to start my own home-based bakery business, "Turner's Treats," here in Melbourne. My passion is bringing joy to people through my handcrafted cakes, pastries, and bread, all made with the finest ingredients and a lot of love.
